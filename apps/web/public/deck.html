Berberica

Bookings without the back-and-forth — a multi-tenant platform for barbers, salons, and beauty studios.

Hackathon 2026 · Built in Skopje · Live in EN + Македонски
The problem

Bookings are still broken.

For an industry worth billions of euros a year, scheduling a haircut still runs on phone tag and Instagram DMs.

73%

Booked over phone / DM

Customers waste their lunch break trying to catch the venue on the line. Venues waste their day answering.

€2K+

Lost per chair, per month

No-shows, double-bookings, and missed slots quietly eat the margin of every small venue.

0

Owners who know last week's revenue

No dashboard, no aggregation, no "which service made the most money". You guess, or you ask the accountant in March.

The solution

One platform. Three jobs done.

Berberica is a customer-facing storefront, a staff-facing scheduler, and an owner-facing P&L — under one roof.

🏪

Storefront in 60 seconds

Every venue gets a branded booking page with its own logo, colors, and address — at /your-name.

📱

Bookings that just work

Real-time slots, email-verified guests, no double-bookings. Mobile-first — the booking flow looks native on a phone.

💰

Earnings on demand

"How much did we make this week? Which barber is top?" Answered in one tap from the Today screen.

What's already built

Production-ready, day one.

Not a prototype — every feature below works in the live app.

✓
Real-time availability engine Exclusion-constraint enforced. No double-booking is possible at the DB level.
✓
Email verification on every guest booking OTP delivered via Resend. Anti-enumeration, rate-limited, single-use.
✓
One-tap status flow for the barber Mark arrived → completed. Earnings update instantly.
✓
Embedded location map Customers see exactly where they're going. One tap → Google Maps directions.
✓
Earnings dashboard Earned vs projected vs lost. Top services + top staff. Daily bar chart.
✓
Bilingual UI English + Македонски. Switch in one tap. Cookies remember per session.
✓
Per-tenant branding Upload your own logo. Set your brand colors. Storefront takes them automatically.
✓
Tenant isolation Every API call is scoped to the resolved tenant. Cross-venue leaks are structurally impossible.
